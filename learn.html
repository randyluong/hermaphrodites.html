Gender-changing Animals(Hermaphrodites)

There are a few different types of hermaphroditisms that include Simultaneous Hermaphroditism, this is an organism that possesses both male and female reproductive organs at the same time. Many invertebrates, such as earthworms, snails, and some fish, exhibit this type. Sequential Hermaphroditism is an organism changes its sex at some point during its life cycle. This can be further divided into two types. Protandry which is an organism that starts life as a male and later changes to a females. Examples include the clownfish. Protogyny is an organism that begins life as a female and later becomes a male. Examples include the sheepshead.

Hermaphrodite Plants

Many flowering plants are hermaphroditic, having flowers that contain both stamens (male reproductive organs) and pistils (female reproductive organs). This adaptation allows for self-pollination, although cross-pollination is often encouraged to maintain genetic diversity.
